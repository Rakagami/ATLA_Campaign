**Earth Brace**

**Level 1 Slot**
- **Type:** Reaction.
- **Range:** self and every creature within [[Earthbending Slot]] *1 Meters of you
- **Effect:**  
	- Root yourself and every creature to resist forced movement.
	- Duration: 1 round.

**Usage**: Ideal for maintaining position in combat.
