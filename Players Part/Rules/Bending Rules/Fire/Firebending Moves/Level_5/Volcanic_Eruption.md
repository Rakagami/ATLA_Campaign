# Volcanic Eruption
#firebending #level5

- **Volcanic Eruption**
  - **Action:** 1 Action.
  - **Range:** 40 * [[Firebending Slot]] meters.
  - **Duration:** Instantaneous.
  - **Damage:** 6d10 fire.
  - Create a massive eruption of molten rock and fire at a target point, affecting all creatures in a 30-foot radius.
