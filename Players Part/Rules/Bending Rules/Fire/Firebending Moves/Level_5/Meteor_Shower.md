# Meteor Shower
#firebending #level5

- **Meteor Shower**
  - **Action:** 2 Actions.
  - **Range:** 50 * [[Firebending Slot]] meters.
  - **Duration:** 3 rounds.
  - Summon fiery meteors that fall in a 50-foot radius. Each round, creatures in the area must make Dexterity saving throws or take 4d6 fire damage.
