#### **Piercing Water (1st Level Move)**

- **Cost:** 1-3 Water Charge.
- **Type:** Action.
- **Range:** 15 meter.
- **Effect:**  
    The waterbender shoots a sharp, pressurized stream of water at a target.
    - **Attack Roll:** [[Int]] + [[Proficiency]].
    - **Damage:** 1d8 piercing damage per Charge.
