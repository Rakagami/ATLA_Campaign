#### **Ice Wall (3rd Level Move)**

- **Cost:** 4 Water Charges.
- **Type:** Action.
- **Range:** 60 ft.
- **Effect:**  
    The waterbender creates a solid wall of ice, up to 30ft long 10 ft tall and 10 ft wide.
    - **HP:** 30 HP.
    - **AC:** 12.
    - **Duration:** 1 minute.
